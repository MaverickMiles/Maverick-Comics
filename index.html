    A Short Poem
    Who am I? I...Am...Iron Man!
    Just kidding, I haven't really figured that out quite yet.
    You can call me Will.
    Everyday I try to understand the implications of my identities, both self-imposed and inherited.
     I try to aspire to the ideal version of the character I have adopted.
     I live to understand what's worth living for.
     Some day, I will figure it out and hatch out of the egg.
     Then I will find the right words to express them,
     and they will be simple.
    Welcome to my studio!
     Here, there are no rules.
    Just keep applying those brush-strokes until something comes out of it.
     The science of art is yet to be explored.
